Flappy Bird

Tap or press Space to flap.

How to Play:
  • Tap / Click / Space to flap upward.
  • Pass pipes to score points. One point per pipe.
  • Press P to pause. New Game restarts.

Controls: Space/Click/Tap to flap. P to pause.
